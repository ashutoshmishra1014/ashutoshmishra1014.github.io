Hi. I'm Ashutosh Mishra.

I am a Machine Learning/Deep Learning enthusiast. I have an affinity towards Artificial Intelligence (specifically Machine Learning and Deep Learning) and I aim at contributing as much as I can to these fields. My research interests are Sequence Learning, Generative Adversarial Networks, Sequence to Sequence Models, Siamese Network, Natural Language Understanding using Deep Learning, and Handwriting Recognition in Air. My current research project is on Visual Question Answering using Multi Modal Reasoning. Apart from my interests in AI I have a keen interest in Web Application development, Music Composition and Music Production.

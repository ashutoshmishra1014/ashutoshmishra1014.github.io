Hi. I'm Ashutosh Mishra.

I am a Machine Learning/Deep Learning practitioner. I have an affinity towards Artificial Intelligence (specifically mathematical interpretation of Machine Learning concepts) and I aim at contributing as much as I can to these fields. My research interests are Learning Optimization of Neural Networks, Computer Vision, Attention based Networks, Variational Auto Encoders, Natural Language Understanding using Deep Learning, and Multi-Modal Fusion Learning. My current research project is on applying attention mechanism for fusing deep features respective to multi-modalities available to vehicles for autonomous navigation. Apart from my interests in AI I have a keen interest in Web Application development, listening to soft music and playing football.
